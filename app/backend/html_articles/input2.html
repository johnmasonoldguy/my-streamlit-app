Rabbit Dental Cleaning Procedure

Abstract: Step-by-step guide to perform routine dental cleaning in rabbits.

Prerequisites

  • Rabbit fasting 2–4 hours prior
  • Anesthesia machine checked
  • Dental tools sterilized

Context

For rabbits showing signs of malocclusion, drooling, or reduced appetite.

Steps

  1. Induce Anesthesia

    Mask rabbit with isoflurane at 2-3%

    Ensure monitoring of respiration and heart rate.

  2. Inspect Oral Cavity

    Check for overgrown incisors or molars.

    Example: Use small speculum and light source.

  3. Trim Teeth

    Use burr or dental files to shorten overgrown teeth

    Teeth properly aligned, rabbit comfortable.

  4. Recovery

    Remove mask and monitor for 10–15 minutes.

Expected Result

Rabbit teeth aligned, normal eating behavior resumed.

Post-Requisites

  • Provide soft food for 24 hours
  • Check for signs of pain or infection
