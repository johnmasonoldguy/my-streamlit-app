Exotic Pet Enclosure Cleaning

Abstract: Proper cleaning and disinfection of reptile and amphibian enclosures.

Prerequisites

  • Wear gloves and mask
  • Remove pets temporarily
  • Prepare disinfectant

Context

Applicable for clinics and homes with snakes, lizards, or amphibians.

Steps

  1. Remove Substrate

    Empty cage, remove bedding and décor

    Enclosure empty, ready for cleaning.

  2. Disinfect Surfaces

    Apply 10% bleach solution or veterinary-approved disinfectant

    Contact time: 10 minutes.

  3. Rinse and Dry

    Rinse thoroughly with water and air dry.

    Example: Ensure no chemical residue remains.

  4. Reassemble Enclosure

    Replace substrate, décor, and return pets.

Expected Result

Safe and clean environment; animals show normal behavior.

Post-Requisites

  • Wash hands and gloves
  • Log cleaning date
