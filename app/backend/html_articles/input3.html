Canine Surgical Prep

Abstract: Preparing dogs for elective surgery including sterilization or mass removal.

Prerequisites

  • Dog fasted 12 hours
  • IV catheter placed
  • Pre-anesthetic bloodwork complete

Context

Applicable for small animal clinics performing sterile surgeries.

Steps

  1. Shave Surgical Site

    Clip hair around incision area

    Minimizes risk of infection.

  2. Disinfect Area

    Apply povidone-iodine followed by alcohol

    Skin disinfected, ready for draping.

  3. Drape Patient

    Place sterile drape, leaving only incision site exposed

    Example: Use towel clamps to secure drape.

  4. Verify Instruments

    All surgical tools sterilized and counted.

Expected Result

Dog ready for surgery under sterile conditions.

Post-Requisites

  • Monitor vitals during surgery
  • Maintain aseptic field throughout
