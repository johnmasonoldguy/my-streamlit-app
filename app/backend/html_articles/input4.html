Abstract: Administer insulin to diabetic cats safely at home or in clinic.

Prerequisites

  • Blood glucose monitored
  • Insulin stored correctly (refrigerated)
  • Syringe prepared

Context

For cats diagnosed with diabetes, requiring daily insulin injection.

Steps

  1. Prepare Injection

    Draw exact dose into syringe

    Check vial for expiration and cloudiness.

  2. Restrain Cat

    Wrap in towel gently, exposing scruff.

    Example: Assistant holds cat, technician prepares injection.

  3. Inject Insulin

    Inject subcutaneously at scruff

    Cat calm, no bleeding or swelling at site.

  4. Record Dose

    Update medical log with dose, time, and glucose reading.

Expected Result

Blood glucose remains within target range; cat stable.

Post-Requisites

  • Monitor for hypoglycemia
  • Schedule next dose
  • Educate owner on signs of low blood sugar
